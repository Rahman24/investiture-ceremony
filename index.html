Investiture ceremony for the student office bearers
BSc degree program
Investiture ceremony

This is the coverage of the Investiture ceremony held on May 24, 2021 at 1800 IST

The program was scheduled to start at 1800 IST. Due to some technical issues, it was delayed by 30 minutes

Welcome address:

Hariharan Balasubramanian started the show with the welcome address. He welcomed all the students and student office bearers of IIT Madras BSc degree program. He then invited Prof. Andrew Thangaraj and Prof. Prathap Haridoss who are the coordinators of the BSc degree program to start the ceremony with their aspiring words.

Andrew's note:

Prof. Andrew Thangaraj showed at the next moment. Before starting his speech, he apologized for the technical difficulties they had due to which the program started late. He thanked everyone for join this event. He stated that he believed that students learn a lot by organizing themselves and being a part of the program administratively and academically. He also stated that students had contributed tremendously in the administrative sense of running the program which is considered as a hallmark of the program. He then concluded with few words with which he handed the stage to Prof. Prathap Haridoss.

Pratap's note:

Prof. Prathap Haridoss started his words by describing his roles in IIT Madras and by welcoming all. He said that it is a tradition of IIT Madras to have students participating in both academic activities and administrative aspects. He also stated that they believed that the overall growth of their students depends on both the technical skills and leadership qualities. Ne concluded his speech saying that he was delighted to acknowledge that the program is going through a right path.

The conversation:

After his aspiring speech, Umma Khateeb, student of this BSc program, showed up to thank Prof. Pratap Haridoss for his words. She then played a conversation between herself and Owaish Shaikh about this program.

The conversation took place with a scenario of Owaish Shaikh enqiuring about the BSc degree program that IIT Madras offers and Umma Khateeb explains everything about the program to him including the course structure, course instructors, the design, the student community and balancing her time with her oncampus degree. Meanwhile Owaish Shaikh enrolled in the program and he exclaimed that the program is amazing. Then she started explaining about the houses and groups we have in this program, including the structure of groups and houses, the election processes to elect group leaders, mentors and upper house council members, with which the conversation ended.

Introduction of houses | The video:

After the conversation, Umma Khateeb started with a famous quote of Nelson Mandela, "Students of today are the leaders of tomorrow" and asked the support team to play the videos of 12 houses that was created as a result of hard work of many students across the houses. Then someone started streaming the videos of 12 houses. While streaming in YouTube, there was some technical glitches and so the team played only the video of Saranda house and stopped streaming. Andrew sir suggested that they'll get back to the video after the special guest's speech.

Prof. Nilesh's profile:

Then Hariharan introduced the Dean of Students at IIT Madras, Prof. Nilesh Vasa who is an alumnus of VJTI Mumbai. He completed his BE in Production Engineering in 1988 and M.Tech in Production Engineering in 1990. He completed his Doctrate of Engineering in Kyosho University, Japan in 1997. He started his journey in IIT Madras as a faculty me mber in the department of Engineering ad design in 2005 and was made the head of the department in 2010. He was made the Dean of Student in 2020.

Prof. Nilesh's words:

Prof. Nilesh Vasa started his words by thanking the moderators of the ceremony, Hariharan Balasubramanian and Umma Khateeb. He exclaimed that the structure of the houses that the students working to bring out was very interesting. He compared the houses of this BSc degree program with the hostels inside IIT Madras, the hostels were named after the rivers of India and the houses are named after the forests of India. He stated that the academics is very important for everyone's growth. He said that the idea of leaders is fascinating as it keeps all the students engaged with the leaders who are engaged with the upper house councils and the support team. He advised the students to involve themselves in the activities and events organized in the houses and also to be a part of a club of their, which is a part of every house, which will give a student some happy memories. He then talked about the hard times that one would be facing and the things that can be done by them to get rid of those times. He then continued with explaining the Wellness Center inside IIT Madras and some important profiles whom the student should know. After delighting the audience with his inspiring words, he concluded his speech with thanking the entire audience and the support team for this opportunity.

Prof. Sivakumar's profile:

After his speech, Hariharan thanked for his words and Umma Khateeb started to introduce the other important guest of the show, Prof. Sivakumar M. Srinivasan, who is a professor in the department of Applied Mechanics at IIT Madras with a past experience of 25 years. He is also a visiting professor at California Institute of Technology, California, USA and at Texas A&M University, Texas, USA. He has also a founder and director of two startups. He started a program called "Engineering in everyday life" for IIT Madras students. He is the person behind many initiatives that were introduced in IIT Madras.

Prof. Sivakumar's words:

Prof. Srinivasan started with explaining the structure of houses of BSc program and hostel councils and executive wings inside the IIT Madras campus and also suggested some additions to the structure of the program and then he continued some future plans for this program. He explained the cultures that are followed oncampus, the roles of leaders and executive members, some initiatives and startups inside IITM. He came up with many ideas to improve and develop the structure of houses and groups which in turns develop the quality of students. He also shared some of his experience as a student and also as a faculty. "One needs to move forward towards a cooperative world", he said. He finished his speech with some aspiring words, "The best way to predict the future is to make one. So, engineer your version of tomorrow with this wonderful opportunity" and wished luck to everyone.

The video again :)

Both the moderators thanked the great speaker and Umma Khateeb apologized once again for the network lag that happened then and asked the team to play it again. Suddenly Prof. Andrew showed up to thank the great speakers for their aspiring speech.

There came the show, the videos made by all the houses was streamed once again sound and clear.

The Intro show:

After the videos, there was an introduction party of all the members of Upper House council which includes the secretaries, deputy secretaries, web admins and an elected mentor for the upper house council from all the houses.

End note:

A vote of thanks was given by Umma Khateeb. She thanked the special guests, the support team and all the audience. She specially thanked the secretaries of all the houses. She then invited Prof. Andrew Thangaraj for his final note. Prof. Andrew said that he really enjoyed the show and appreciated the hard work of the profiles behind the making of videos and conduction of the ceremony and then asked Prof Prathap Haridoss for his final note. Prof. Pratap advised everyone to prioritize things and he remembered the Bandipur Times newspaper and Prof. Andrew's hilarious reply to that. He advised everyone to prioritize things and once again thanked everyone for being the part of this amazing event.

With his words, a two hour long show came to an end
